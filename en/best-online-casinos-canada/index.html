Is online gambling legal in Canada?

Yes, online gambling is legal in Canada. Ontario has a fully regulated private market through iGaming Ontario (AGCO) with over 49 licensed operators. Players in all other provinces can legally use offshore-licensed casinos. No Canadian federal or provincial law criminalises individual players for gambling at licensed offshore sites.

Ontario launched its regulated private market in April 2022. British Columbia operates PlayNow.com, Quebec runs EspaceJeux and Alberta has PlayAlberta. Outside these provincial platforms, casinos licensed under Curaçao eGaming or the Kahnawake Gaming Commission are legally accessible across Canada. Ontario players get the strongest consumer protection by using only iGO-licensed operators — Jackpot City and Spin Casino in our rankings.
