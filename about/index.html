Our review methodology

Every casino on CasinoScout is scored across eight criteria. The overall score is a weighted average, with payout reliability and licence quality carrying the most weight. A casino that fails our licence or payout tests is not listed regardless of commission rates.

01
Licence verification
We verify the licence directly with the issuing authority — Curaçao, MGA, Kahnawake or iGaming Ontario. We check for active status, not just claims on the casino's website.
02
Deposit testing
We make real deposits using Interac, crypto and card at every casino we review. We verify deposit speed, minimum amounts and whether Canadian-currency accounts are available.
03
Withdrawal testing
We request real withdrawals and time them. Any casino that delays beyond stated withdrawal times, or adds unexpected fees, is penalised in the payout score.
04
Bonus terms analysis
We read the full bonus terms and calculate true value in CAD. We score wagering requirements, game contribution weights, maximum bet limits and expiry periods.
05
Provincial eligibility
We test each casino from Canadian IP addresses across multiple provinces. Ontario availability is verified separately against the iGO licensed operator list.
06
Responsible gambling tools
We check for deposit limits, self-exclusion, reality checks, session time limits and links to Canadian support organisations. Absent tools lower the score.
07
Player complaint monitoring
We monitor Casinomeister, AskGamblers and Canadian gambling forums for unresolved complaints. Multiple unresolved complaints trigger a review and potential removal.
08
Quarterly updates
All ratings are reviewed every 90 days. Casinos that fall below standards — through changed terms, licence issues or payout problems — are downgraded or removed.
