Our review methodology

Every casino in our rankings goes through the same eight-point testing process. We do not publish a review until all eight steps are complete.

1. Licence verification
Weight: critical
We verify the licence directly with the issuing authority — Kahnawake Gaming Commission, iGaming Ontario (AGCO) or Curaçao eGaming. We do not rely on the casino's own claims.
2. Deposit testing
Weight: high
We make real deposits via Interac e-Transfer and cryptocurrency from Canadian bank accounts. We verify funds credit correctly and at the stated speed.
3. Withdrawal timing
Weight: critical
We time withdrawals from the moment of request to confirmed arrival in our account. We run multiple tests at different times of day and week to get accurate averages.
4. Bonus term analysis
Weight: high
We read the full bonus terms — not marketing copy. We calculate effective wagering requirements including game contribution rates, max bet rules and time limits.
5. Provincial availability
Weight: critical
We verify Ontario licence status with iGaming Ontario directly. We test access from Ontario, BC and Alberta IPs. Casinos are clearly labelled for which provinces they serve.
6. Game library review
Weight: medium
We play across slots, live dealer and table games. We verify RTPs match published figures and check that demo modes work without registration.
7. Customer support test
Weight: medium
We contact live chat at different hours with genuine questions. We measure response time and assess the quality and accuracy of answers provided.
8. Complaint monitoring
Weight: high
We monitor player complaints on Casinomeister, AskGamblers and relevant forums monthly. Casinos with patterns of unresolved complaints are removed from our rankings.
