Is online gambling legal in Canada?

Yes, online gambling is legal in Canada. Ontario operates a fully regulated private market through iGaming Ontario. Players in other provinces can legally play at offshore-licensed casinos — no Canadian law prohibits individual players from doing so.

Do you pay tax on casino winnings in Canada?

No — in most cases, Canadian players do not pay tax on casino winnings. The CRA treats recreational gambling winnings as non-taxable windfalls. Tax only applies if gambling is your primary source of income, which is rare for recreational players.

This applies whether you play at an Ontario-licensed casino or an offshore-licensed site. Winnings are not reported on your personal tax return for casual play.
